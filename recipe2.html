PANEER BUTTER MASALA

Servings:

3–4

Ingredients:

  • 200-250g paneer cubed
  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1.5 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 2–3 tbsp cream
  • 1/2 tsp dried fenugreek leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fry paneer until golden and soak in warm water.
  2. Sauté cumin seeds in oil + butter, add onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook till raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add water, simmer, then blend if smoother texture desired.
  6. Add cream, fenugreek, garam masala, and simmer with paneer.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

SOFT VANILLA CAKE

Servings:

8

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup softened butter
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a cake tin.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one by one, mix well, and add vanilla.
  4. Sift and mix dry ingredients in.
  5. Alternate milk and dry mix while stirring gently.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes. Let cool and enjoy!

CREAMY MIXED FRUIT SMOOTHIE

Servings:

2

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • ½ cup mango or other sweet fruit
  • ½ cup berries (optional)
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ½ cup milk (adjust as needed)
  • 1–2 tsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until creamy.
  3. Serve chilled with ice cubes if desired.
